BobPool Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 24, 2025

Last Modified: September 24, 2025

Key Information Summary

  • Information Collected: Email, nickname, service usage records
  • Purpose of Use: Service provision, personalized content delivery
  • Retention Period: Until membership withdrawal
  • Third-party Sharing: Supabase (backend service)

Article 1 (Purpose of Personal Information Processing)

BobPool ("Company" or "Service") processes personal information for the following purposes. The personal information being processed will not be used for purposes other than those specified below, and when the purpose of use changes, necessary measures will be implemented, such as obtaining separate consent in accordance with Article 18 of the Personal Information Protection Act.

1. Membership Registration and Management

  • Confirmation of membership registration intention, identification and authentication for membership services
  • Maintenance and management of membership qualifications, prevention of unauthorized service use
  • Processing personal information for various notices, notifications, and complaint handling

2. Goods or Service Provision

  • Recipe sharing service provision, content delivery
  • Personalized service provision, payment processing and settlement
  • Processing personal information for identity verification, purchase and payment processing

3. Marketing and Advertising Utilization

  • Development of new services (products) and provision of customized services
  • Provision of events and advertising information and participation opportunities
  • Processing personal information for service validity verification and access frequency analysis

Article 2 (Personal Information Processing and Retention Period)

① The Company processes and retains personal information within the personal information retention and use period according to law or the personal information retention and use period agreed upon when collecting personal information from data subjects.

② The specific personal information processing and retention periods are as follows:

1. Membership Registration and Management: Until membership withdrawal

However, in the following cases, until the end of the relevant period:

  • When investigation or prosecution is necessary due to violation of related laws: until the end of such investigation
  • When creditor-debtor relationships remain due to service use: until settlement of such relationships

2. Recipe and Cooking Records: 1 year after membership withdrawal

Stored in anonymized form for service quality improvement and statistical analysis

3. Legally Required Data Storage

  • Records of contracts or withdrawal of subscription offers: 5 years (E-commerce Act)
  • Records of payment and supply of goods: 5 years (E-commerce Act)
  • Records of consumer complaints or dispute processing: 3 years (E-commerce Act)
  • Website visit records: 3 months (Protection of Communications Secrets Act)

Article 3 (Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties)

① The Company processes personal information only within the scope specified in Article 1 (Purpose of Personal Information Processing) and provides personal information to third parties only when it falls under Article 17 of the Personal Information Protection Act, such as consent from data subjects or special provisions of law.

1. Supabase Inc.

  • Recipient: Supabase Inc.
  • Purpose of recipient's personal information use: Backend service provision, database management
  • Personal information items provided: Member information, recipe data, usage records
  • Recipient's retention and use period: Service use period or until membership withdrawal
  • Legal basis: Data subject consent

Article 4 (Items of Personal Information Processed)

The Company processes the following personal information items:

1. Membership Registration and Management

  • Required items: Email address, password, nickname
  • Optional items: Profile picture, self-introduction

2. Information Automatically Generated During Service Use

  • IP address, cookies, service usage records, access logs
  • Device information (OS type, browser information, screen size, etc.)

Article 5 (Measures to Ensure Safety of Personal Information)

The Company implements the following technical, administrative, and physical measures necessary to ensure safety in accordance with Article 29 of the Personal Information Protection Act:

1. Encryption of Personal Information

Users' personal information passwords are encrypted and stored so that only the individual can know them, and important data uses separate security functions such as file and transmission data encryption or file locking functions.

2. Technical Measures Against Hacking

To prevent personal information leakage and damage by hacking or computer viruses, security programs are installed and regularly updated and checked. Systems are installed in areas with controlled external access and are technically and physically monitored and blocked.

3. Minimization and Training of Personal Information Processing Staff

Personal information processing staff are designated and limited to those in charge, implementing measures to manage personal information by minimizing it.

Article 6 (Rights and Obligations of Data Subjects and Exercise Methods)

① Data subjects may exercise the following personal information protection-related rights against the Company at any time:

  1. Request for notification of personal information processing status
  2. Request for access to personal information
  3. Request for correction and deletion of personal information
  4. Request for suspension of personal information processing

② The exercise of rights under paragraph 1 may be made to the Company through written documents, telephone, email, fax, etc., and the Company will take action without delay.
